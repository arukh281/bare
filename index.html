General notes

BBRE Commercial opened its desk in 2003, when Gurgaon's office corridors were still being drawn. We have worked the same market ever since — for the businesses taking space, and for the owners who provide it. We have never treated those as separate trades: a tenant's brief teaches you what a building is worth; an owner's mandate teaches you what a tenant will sign.

That double apprenticeship is the firm. It is why our shortlists are honest, our negotiations short, and why — twenty-two years in — the party across the table is rarely a stranger.

Part I · For occupiers

From brief to handover.

  1. 01

    Understand your requirement

    We map team size, budget, location and timelines, so the brief is right before we show a single option.

  2. 02

    Share relevant proposals

    A curated shortlist of Grade-A options that genuinely fit — no noise, only real contenders.

  3. 03

    Arrange site tours

    We coordinate viewings across your shortlist so you compare spaces like for like.

  4. 04

    Test layout & design

    A test-fit layout and design for the shortlisted office, so you can picture your team in it.

  5. 05

    Negotiate & execute the lease

    We negotiate commercial terms and see the lease through to signature.

  6. 06

    Build & handover

    A tailor-made office, fitted out and handed over ready to occupy.
