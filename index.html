General notes

BBRE Commercial opened its desk in 2003, when Gurgaon's office corridors were still being drawn. We have worked the same market ever since — for the businesses taking space, and for the owners who provide it. We have never treated those as separate trades: a tenant's brief teaches you what a building is worth; an owner's mandate teaches you what a tenant will sign.

That double apprenticeship is the firm. It is why our shortlists are honest, our negotiations short, and why — twenty-two years in — the party across the table is rarely a stranger.
